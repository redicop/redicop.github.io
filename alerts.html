How Alerts Work

1

Choose Your Data Types

Select which categories of remote sensing data you want alerts for — satellite optical, SAR, LiDAR, aerial, or emergency activations.

2

Define Your Area of Interest

Specify your geographic region of interest. Alerts are filtered to data relevant to your area using nltGIS.ai spatial intelligence.

3

Get Notified

Receive GitHub notifications when new data is available, emergency charters are activated, or satellite passes are scheduled over your AOI.

4

Access & Analyze

Follow direct links to data portals, download imagery, and use nltGIS.ai to query and visualize data for your disaster response needs.
