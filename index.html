About

What is REDICOP?

The Remote Sensing in Disasters Community of Practice (REDICOP), formerly the Remote Sensing Innovation Working Group (RSIWG), is co-organized by MIT Lincoln Laboratory and New Light Technologies.

REDICOP bridges remote sensing research and emergency management operations. We curate data sources, share best practices, host community meetings, and provide real-time awareness of satellite, aerial, and drone data availability for disaster response.

Our platform leverages nltGIS.ai spatial intelligence to deliver data alerts, geospatial queries, and community tools.
