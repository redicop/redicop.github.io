About REDICOP

What is REDICOP?

REDICOP is a collaboration between New Light Technologies and MIT Lincoln Laboratory to foster innovation on the use of remote sensing in disaster response and recovery.

Our community brings together researchers, practitioners, and decision-makers to develop guidelines, standards, and workflows that enable emergency managers to effectively use remote sensing technologies and products.

Register to attend our July 2025 virtual meeting on hurricane season preparedness. The session will feature presentations on the latest remote sensing capabilities and lessons learned from recent hurricane response operations.
