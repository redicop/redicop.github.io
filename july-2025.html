July 2025 Meeting

Preparing for the 2025 Hurricane Season

Meeting Details

📅July 10, 2025
🕒1:00 - 2:00 PM ET
💻Virtual (Zoom) - Link TBD

About This Meeting

This quarterly REDICOP meeting will focus on preparing for the 2025 Atlantic hurricane season. Topics include pre-positioning satellite tasking, SAR-based flood mapping workflows, coordination between federal and commercial imagery providers, and lessons learned from the 2024 hurricane season response.
